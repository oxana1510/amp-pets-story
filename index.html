How to Disable the “WhatsApp Web is Currently Active”Notification on Android

Open your phone’s Settings menu by tapping the settings icon on the main menu

Navigate to Apps and Notifications and tap it

Find WhatsApp in the list of applications that appear on the page and tap on the WhatsApp icon

Tap on Notifications. Under Other, uncheck Other notifications and Silent notifications

The notification “WhatsApp Web is now active”will be disabled and won’t appear in the notification bar
